  What a pixel map is and what makes one look good.
- `foundations`
  The knowledge and tools you need from beginner level.

  A practical learning path and time-based study plan.
- `project`
  Notes specifically about your current `demo` app.
- `practice`
  Exercises, mistakes to avoid, and a quality checklist.

## Important

Your current `demo` app is a graph-based indoor map renderer, not yet a full handcrafted pixel-art tilemap workflow.

That means you need to learn two things in parallel:

- pixel art and tilemap design
- rendering, data structures, and tooling
